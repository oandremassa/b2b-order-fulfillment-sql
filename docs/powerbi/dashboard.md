# Dashboard page

The report contains one page named `Overview`.

## Filters

- Country
- Customer type
- Sales channel
- Warehouse
- Date

## Key figures

- Net revenue
- Completed orders
- Average order value
- Gross margin rate
- Repeat customer rate

## Visuals

| Visual | Fields |
|---|---|
| Monthly net revenue | Year-month and net revenue |
| Revenue by sales channel | Sales channel, net revenue and margin-rate tooltip |
| Revenue by customer type | Customer type and net revenue |
| Fulfillment by warehouse | Completed orders and on-time delivery rate |
| Products by revenue | Product and net revenue |
| Return rate by category | Product category and return rate |
| Inventory status | Product, warehouse, stock, reorder level and status |

The page is intentionally limited to one operational overview. Product-level and customer-level drillthrough pages are outside the current scope.
